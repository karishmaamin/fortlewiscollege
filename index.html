    Whether you already know what career you’d enjoy or are curious to figure it out as you go along, FLC’s academic programs provide the perfect balance of structure and freedom. Our primary focuses include business, health and technical sciences, education, creative arts, humanities, and place-based studies that build on FLC’s unique location.

From orientation through to graduation, your advising team will help you select courses, choose your major, and graduate prepared to start your career.
